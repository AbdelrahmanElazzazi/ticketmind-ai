Invoice Request Procedure

Customers may request copies of historical invoices, tax invoices, payment confirmations, and billing statements. Invoice requests are handled through the billing support workflow.

Invoices are stored securely and can be accessed through the customer portal. Customers should first attempt self-service retrieval before contacting support.

When handling invoice requests, support agents must verify account ownership and ensure that requested documents belong to the customer.

Common Requests:

* Monthly invoice copies
* Annual billing summaries
* Tax documentation
* Payment receipts
* Subscription billing history

Process:

1. Verify customer identity.
2. Confirm invoice period requested.
3. Retrieve invoice from billing records.
4. Deliver invoice securely.
5. Document request completion.

Invoices are generally retained for seven years unless otherwise specified by company policy.

Expected Resolution Time:
Invoice requests are typically completed within four business hours.
