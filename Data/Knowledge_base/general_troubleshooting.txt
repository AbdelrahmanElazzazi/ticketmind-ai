General Troubleshooting Guide

General troubleshooting procedures should be followed whenever the root cause of an issue is unclear.

Initial Assessment:

1. Gather issue details.
2. Identify affected users.
3. Determine business impact.
4. Review recent changes.
5. Check service status.

Standard Troubleshooting Workflow:

* Reproduce the issue.
* Review application logs.
* Verify user permissions.
* Confirm network connectivity.
* Validate browser compatibility.
* Test alternative workflows.

Escalation Criteria:

* System-wide impact
* Security incidents
* Data integrity concerns
* Infrastructure failures
* Third-party service outages

Documentation Requirements:

All troubleshooting activities, findings, and resolutions must be documented within the ticketing system for future reference and knowledge sharing.

Expected Resolution Time:
Varies depending on incident severity and complexity.
