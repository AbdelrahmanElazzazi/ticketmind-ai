Subscription Upgrade Guide

Customers may upgrade subscription plans to access additional features, increased usage limits, enhanced support services, and enterprise functionality.

Subscription upgrades become effective immediately after successful payment confirmation. Billing adjustments are calculated using prorated pricing whenever applicable.

Common Upgrade Requests:

* Basic to Professional
* Professional to Enterprise
* User license expansion
* Feature package activation

Upgrade Procedure:

1. Review current subscription.
2. Confirm target plan.
3. Verify pricing.
4. Process billing adjustment.
5. Confirm activation.

Customers should review plan features carefully before upgrading to ensure alignment with business requirements.

Expected Resolution Time:
Most upgrades are completed within 15 minutes.
